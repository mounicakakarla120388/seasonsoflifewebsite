About

Seasons of Life Consulting

by Eleanor Bently

Over 30 years experience in intentional daily lifestyle habits with a focus in body, mind and spirit. Over 27 years experience in home economics, child development and home education and a lifetime of fitness and exercise principles that have kept me lean and healthy for more than 50 years.
I received my formal training from the Institute for Integrative Nutrition*, where I studied a variety of dietary theories and practical lifestyle coaching methods. With my knowledge and personal experience, we co-create completely personalized actions based on your goals to move you toward your ideal vision of health within your unique body, lifestyle, preferences, and resources.
